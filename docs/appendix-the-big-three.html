The Economy & Us

Chapter 2 Appendix: The Big Three

2.1 Purpose & Learning Objectives

The purpose of this appendix is to explicitly describe the three primary measures of the health of the economy: GDP, Unemployment, and Inflation. These appendix will be brief and concise. It is focused solely on clarify definitions and helping reinforce the distinction between “levels” and “rates”.

When students have completed reading this appendix, they will be able to:

  • Define GDP, Unemployment, and Inflation in specific and intuitive terms.
  • Visualize GDP, Unemployment, the Unemployment Rate, and Inflation using the publicly available data.
  • Be able to explore these data at multiple geographic scales.

This appendix will prepare students for Data Viz #2: Measuring your economy.
